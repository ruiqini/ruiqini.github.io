Biography

I am a PhD student in the Deparment of Computer Science at Purdue University, supervised by Prof. Ahmed Qureshi.

I was a graduate student in the Deparment of Computer Science at Florida State University, supervised by Prof. Xifeng Gao, and I also work closely with Dr. Zherong Pan. I had an internship at Adobe Research, working with Dr. Kevin Wampler in 2021 summer.

I received my B.S degree from University of Science and Technology of China in 2018, supervised by Prof. Ligang Liu and Dr. Xiao-Ming Fu.

Motion Planning and Control, Trajectory Optimization, Physics-based Simulation, Geometry Processing
